Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This page describes how Ground Truth collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers from authentication providers (e.g., Apple or Google sign-in).
  • Your selected news source preferences.
  • Device notification token used for push notification delivery.
  • App usage and basic diagnostic metadata needed for service reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • To personalize your feed based on selected sources.
  • To send push notifications for new matching articles.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve app reliability and performance.

3. Data Storage

  • User preferences are stored in Firebase/Firestore and associated with your user ID.
  • Articles and summaries are stored in PostgreSQL.
  • Push tokens are stored in backend tables for notification delivery.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication and Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (APNs)
  • OpenAI (for article summarization/classification)
  • Railway (backend and database hosting)

5. Your Choices

  • You can update source preferences in the app.
  • You can disable push notifications in iOS Settings.
  • You can request account deletion from within the app.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: info@groundtruthai.app
