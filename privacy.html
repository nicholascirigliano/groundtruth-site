Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Ground Truth provides AI news feeds and personalized source preferences. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how you can control or delete your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account information from sign-in (such as email and display name)
  • Source preferences you select in the app
  • Device push token for notification delivery
  • Basic app usage and error information needed for operation

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate your account
  • To save and load your source preferences
  • To send push notifications for new matching articles
  • To operate, secure, and improve app reliability

Data Storage

User preferences are stored in Firebase/Firestore by user ID. Article and summary data are stored in PostgreSQL. Push tokens are stored to support notification delivery.

Third-Party Services

Ground Truth uses:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (APNs)
  • OpenAI (for article summaries and classification)

Your Controls

  • You can update source preferences at any time in Source Selection.
  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete your account from Profile, which removes your auth account and stored user preferences.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app administrator.
